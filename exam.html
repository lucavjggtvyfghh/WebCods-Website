Teachers

The teacher will have access to the following options –
  • 1. Reports showing the performance of students, with customized segregations.*
  • 2. Reports showing the attendance of students, with customized segregations.*
  • 3. Reports showing how many students have submitted their assignments.
  • 4. Upload tests.
  • 5. Upload assignments
  • 6. Upload study materials
  • 7. Update news / messages on the E-Notice Board.
* The segregation will be as per percentage and school.

Students

The student will have access to the following options –
  • 1. Download study materials
  • 2. Download assignments
  • 3. Upload assignments
  • 4. Appear for online tests.*
  • 5. Read the updates / messages that are given on the E-Notice Board.
*There are certain coaching centers who take tests in the form of test series.
Other coaching centers hold subject-wise tests.
In case of test series, the student will pay Rs 150/- per test series.
In case of subject-wise tests, the student will pay Rs 150/- for a period of 45 days.

Social Shiksha - Online Exam Portal

Main Features –
  • Students can access solved previous year papers from the portal.
  • If there is any new assignment, test or any update; a notification sms can be sent out to teachers and students.
  • If the coaching center/teacher requires, the portal can also provide a payment gateway to the students to pay the fees of the coaching center/teacher.
  • There will be a discussion forum available to both teachers and students. There will be a provision of selecting the audience. The audience selection options will be as follows-
      • For Teachers:
    1. Teachers Only
    2. Students Only
    3. Teachers & Students
    4. Customized list of audience
      For Students:
    1. Students Only
    2. Teachers & Students
    3. Customized list of audience
